Track characters

Select one or more characters to highlight scenes where they appear.

HINTS:
  • To highlight your nine most frequent characters without opening this dialog, use keys 1—9.
    Deselect all highlights with 0 (zero).
  • To select two or more characters simultaneously, hold CMD while pressing the second character name
  • When more than one character is selected, scenes with BOTH (ALL) characters present are highlighted.
  • You can also open (and dismiss) this dialog by pressing T for ”track characters”.


NOTE:
Characters must be speaking at least once to appear in the list. Once they do, they are also recognized when mentioned in an action element.
To avoid false positives with names that are referred to in a scene where they are not present, as in she grabs the gun Frank gave her, adding two asterisks inside a name (Fra**nk) will allow it to pass under the radar without affecting the printed/exported text.
